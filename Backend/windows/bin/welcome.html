RePCC
Remote Personal Computer Control
Installer & Setup Guide

Key Features

  • Control your PC from your Android phone
  • Works seamlessly over your home network
  • Create custom macros for frequent tasks
  • Presentation control & laser pointer
  • Screen mirroring via WebRTC
  • Wake-on-LAN PC startup
  • Simple one-time setup

Quick Start

1. Install This Software

Follow the installer wizard. It will set up the Windows backend service on your PC.

2. Download Mobile App

Install the RePCC Flutter app on your Android device from our project repository.

3. Configure Network

Both devices must be on the same network. The app will automatically discover your PC.

Setup Tutorials

🔧 Initial Setup
  1. Complete the installer wizard
  2. Open Windows Settings → Firewall
  3. Ensure RePCC is allowed through the firewall (both private and public networks)
  4. Note your PC's IP address (Settings → Network & Internet → WiFi → Status)
📱 Connect Your Mobile Device
  1. Install RePCC app on your Android device
  2. Ensure your phone is connected to the same WiFi network
  3. Open the app and tap "Discover PC"
  4. Select your PC from the list that appears
  5. Accept the connection request on your PC (if prompted)
⌨️ Create Custom Macros
  1. In the RePCC app, navigate to Macros
  2. Tap "New Macro" and give it a name
  3. Record mouse movements, clicks, and keyboard inputs
  4. Save the macro
  5. Execute macros anytime with a single tap from your phone
🎤 Presentation Mode
  1. Open your presentation on your PC (PowerPoint, PDF, etc.)
  2. In the RePCC app, select "Presentation Tools"
  3. Use "Next/Previous" buttons to navigate slides
  4. Activate the laser pointer to highlight areas on screen
  5. View presenter notes on your phone while presenting
⚠️ Important: RePCC only works on local networks (LAN). For security, it does not connect over the internet. Both your PC and device must be on the same WiFi network.
